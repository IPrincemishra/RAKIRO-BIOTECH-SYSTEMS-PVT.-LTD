Combination Kits

AQUASOL Combination Water Test Kits are extremely simple and easy to use & make monitoring convenient and quick, thus preventive actions can be taken immediately. To get reliable results from these combination water test kits, you neither have to be a trained chemist nor require any experience. AQUASOL water hardness test kits are based on standard and established analytical methods, backed by chemical research. These are simple adaptations of classic chemical analytical methods. The analytical methods are based on Standard Methods for Examination of Water and Waste Water prepared by the American Public Health Association (APHA), American Water Works Association (AWWA), different pharmacopeias, and other standard methods.

You can have complete confidence in the results you obtain from AQUASOL testing kits for water, as they are extremely reliable. AQUASOL systems replace conventional analytical methods such as Titrimetric, Colorimetric, and Spectrophotometric with simple drop tests & standard colour comparison methods. AQUASOL water testing kits are completely ready to use, conveniently packed, and therefore user-friendly. The drop count procedures and suitable colour comparisons of these total hardness test kits for hard water are also economical. AQUASOL water total hardness test kits are extremely convenient and save you from the tedium of the laboratory while saving precious time. Now you do not have to bother about reagent preparations and standardization, therefore AQUASOL water test kits have been designed especially for you, who require accuracy of the highest standards.

The Aquasol combination kit is available in one single pack for multiple parameters therefore in one single pack various parameters can be analyzed increasing the convenience and Portability.
