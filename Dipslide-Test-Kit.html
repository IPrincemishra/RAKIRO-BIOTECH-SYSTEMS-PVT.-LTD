Dipslide Test Kit

BACTASLYDE DIPSLIDE TEST KIT is a pre-sterilized bacteria dipslide test kit consisting of a plastic paddle coated with specially developed proprietary media on each side for the growth of a variety of bacteria, yeasts & fungi. The dual media of these bacteria dip slide test kits enables you to perform the Total Bacterial Count on one side and the specific bacteria species count on the other side at the same time in just 24 hours. BACTASLYDE water bacteria testers are supplied in boxes of 5 along with the complete instructions set & a density chart to have a complete quantitative analysis of bacteria.

WHAT IS DIPSLIDE?

BACTASLYDE DIPSLIDE TBC Test Kit is a pre-sterilized slide, coated with specially developed media, for the growth of a variety of bacteria, yeasts & fungi. The dual media of this bacteria dip slide test kit enables you to perform different tests, at a time & can be used virtually in any industry. The water Dipslide by BACTASLYDE is reliable and compares well with conventional microbiological techniques. This bacteria detection kit is in regular use, worldwide, for monitoring microbiological contamination.

WHY DIPSLIDE TEST KIT?
  • Avoid breakdowns
  • Optimise usage of water treatment chemicals
  • Improve efficiency
  • Prolong equipment life
  • Provide a healthier working environment
  • Reduce corrosion
WHEN DIPSLIDE COMPARED TO OTHER METHODS?
  • Microbiology laboratory not required
  • Can be used by non-technical people
  • Extremely easy and quick
  • No capital investment
  • Most Economical
  • Can be used at the site very effective
HOW TO USE – DIPSLIDE?
  1. Dip the water bacteria tester (Dip for 20-25 seconds. Use a clean container to hold the test material)
  2. Incubate (At room temperature (18-32 Deg C) No need for an Incubator)
  3. Read (Match with Density Chart provided)
