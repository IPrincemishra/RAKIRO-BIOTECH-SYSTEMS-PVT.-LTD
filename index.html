Rakiro Biotech Systems Pvt. Ltd.

We are glad to introduce ourselves as company that provides solutions for testing, measurement and water treatment in various Industries. In past few years we have made a mark in the field of testing and have presence in India as well as other part of the world with a wide network of dealers & distributors.

Our pragmatic workforce & state of art manufacturing facility manufacture a wide range of Instruments & Field Test Kits for both microbiological & analytical parameters.

Also with a vast experience in the field of water treatment our chemical division specializes in formulation chemicals required in smooth running of industry which helps improve the efficiency of production.

History

In the year 1993, Onsite testing procedures for Microbiological as well as Analytical parameters was tedious & specialized activity. Then four visionary with combined experience joined hands to make life easy & simple for process industry segment by developing onsite easy to use Testing equipments.

They began the journey in small flat of 500 sq feet, with the state of the art product BACTASLYDE, a microbiological testing device which made microbiological testing extremely easy & simple.
